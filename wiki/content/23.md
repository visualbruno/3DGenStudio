# AI (Mask + ComfyUI)

You can do **Inpainting** using a ComfyUI workflow.

Your ComfyUI workflow must have at least 2 inputs of type image.
- Source Image
- Source Mask

![{886B1D3B-085A-4F47-A3B9-D6B24CC27E79}](http://localhost:3001/wiki-media/1780048352690-743611347.png)

Make sur to select the correct dropdown options for both images
![{FBCBD113-D4B8-4E06-AB41-1B76837C4835}](http://localhost:3001/wiki-media/1780048509632-980171500.png)

Draw your mask on the image
![{22F8AFD1-C743-47B7-B8FD-D1B973CE34A3}](http://localhost:3001/wiki-media/1780048403288-247940357.png)

Enter a prompt, like "changes hair color to red" and run the workflow

![image](http://localhost:3001/wiki-media/1780048595618-367471286.png)

Everytime your run a workflow, it generates a new layer on the right.

You can decide to overwrite your image or to save it as a new version.
![image](http://localhost:3001/wiki-media/1780048754071-18178992.png)

Saving it as a new version, the result will be visible in the **Edits** of the image.
![image](http://localhost:3001/wiki-media/1780048811320-533577488.png)
