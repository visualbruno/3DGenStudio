# Projection

Projecting views on a mesh to have a better texture quality.

![image](http://localhost:3001/wiki-media/1780050812474-617303149.png)

1. Click on "Start" button

It will ask you if you want to keep the original texture or to erase it.

2. Select the ComfyUI Workflow

Here I use "Mesh Projection with QwenImageEdit"

![{6DFC4BE1-4D63-44B3-BF90-12153C6E8981}](http://localhost:3001/wiki-media/1780050899383-608716012.png)

Select a "Reference Image", that will be used to draw on the mesh.

Enter a prompt: for the first image, I wrote "Draw the front view"

3. Position your mesh correctly

![{9BB6C539-D9E2-40A9-A4DD-E0C1706B75C2}](http://localhost:3001/wiki-media/1780050977503-653916792.png)

4. Click on "Project View" and wait on the result

![{E4740E7B-F72E-4601-98E8-3DA25361029B}](http://localhost:3001/wiki-media/1780051004954-389032289.png)

5. Look at the result

A new layer is added on the right with different parameters you can change

**Sometimes, ComfyUI can generate weird result. Just delete the layer, change the seed, or the position or your prompt and generate again the view.**

![{A4A6FAC2-99A6-4495-9905-8E91845EDB36}](http://localhost:3001/wiki-media/1780051144899-617866671.png)

6. Let's do the back now

Rotate your mesh and change the prompt to "Draw the back view"
![{BA9FDE93-0DFF-44C0-B3E9-8C3757EB3BCF}](http://localhost:3001/wiki-media/1780051177889-730180928.png)

Click on "Project View" and wait on the result
![{095837EA-71ED-48EE-98FF-69657D140E29}](http://localhost:3001/wiki-media/1780051351090-504573334.png)


7. Doing the sides

That's the tricky part, because now we must deal with "Seams".

Sometimes, it's better to generate a view, not from the left side, but more on the "front-left" side

Don't forget to change the prompt to "Draw the front-left side view"

![{7AF14FA5-E0CC-40F3-9C03-526E1FF3751B}](http://localhost:3001/wiki-media/1780051399103-656893963.png)

Click on "Project View" and wait on the result

8. Post Processing

You can fill holes (not textured part) and smooth the seams

![{21981661-BD8E-4709-9380-EA507A25D581}](http://localhost:3001/wiki-media/1780052443300-284110766.png)
