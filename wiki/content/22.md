# Export your Workflow from ComfyUI

Imaging you want to import a ComfyUI workflow to generate an image with Flux2-Klein 9B.

1. Prepare your workflow

![{E9FA2F6A-BF4A-41D0-85DA-0F9FC015BBFE}](http://localhost:3001/wiki-media/1780046862023-749151920.png)

2. Rename the nodes that you will import in 3D Gen Studio

It will be easier to find them.

3. Export your workflow using the button "Export (API)"
