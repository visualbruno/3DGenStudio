# Images

Import bitmap images, browse them, and open them in the **Image Editor**. Each image can keep a history of edits.

![image](http://localhost:3001/wiki-media/1780045961003-835182047.png)

You can see the blue number with a clock below an image, it's the number of "Edit" you did with this image. Just click on it and you will see the popup about your **Edits**.

![{DBEC9960-F5D9-49C1-9429-B1BAAD8BC00D}](http://localhost:3001/wiki-media/1780046032879-97625514.png)

Rename your images by clicking on the pencil icon.

You can **Open** and **Edit** your image using the **Image Editor**.
