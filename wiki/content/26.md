# Displace

Displace the vertex using a **Brush**

![image](http://localhost:3001/wiki-media/1780049866042-885479357.png)

1. Click on "Place Stamp"

![image](http://localhost:3001/wiki-media/1780049946656-799849994.png)

2. When you are happy with the position, click on your mesh.

Now you have different settings to play with.

**Increase the Tessellation only at the end, because it is very slow**

3. Play with the parameters

![{0D83FA0A-1832-481F-9253-1FC679E1D20B}](http://localhost:3001/wiki-media/1780049997810-945538147.png)

Adjust the **Normal Offset**
![image](http://localhost:3001/wiki-media/1780050093830-492121251.png)

Increase the **Tessellation**
![{E43B27EE-F870-4F12-8BE3-70C77D7DB43A}](http://localhost:3001/wiki-media/1780050212746-337848884.png)

4. Apply Displace

5. Save your mesh
