# Images

You have the choice between
- Local Computer
- From Assets
- ComfyUI Workflow
- Remote API

![{BE426B60-AA3E-4778-9FAC-1B488269E85C}](http://localhost:3001/wiki-media/1780043869336-36601302.png)

## Local Computer
Choose a picture from your computer, it will be automatically added to your Assets.

## From Assets
A Selector popup will appear, choose one of your imported assets.
![{F5F33261-4BBD-4320-BC92-A8B6F8D2FF75}](http://localhost:3001/wiki-media/1780043972576-818411108.png)

## ComfyUI Workflow
Choose one of your ComfyUI Workflow, fill the fields and "START WORKFLOW".
![{FB79705D-2707-4D61-AB25-549221C92A34}](http://localhost:3001/wiki-media/1780044069389-611177715.png)
In case of success, the image will appear in the card and automatically added to your Assets.
![{735BA1C4-28F2-4C9C-849E-29F430C96F11}](http://localhost:3001/wiki-media/1780044122464-66667004.png)

## Remote API
Choose one of available external API, enter the prompt and wait for the result.

**You must enter your own API key at the Settings page**

![{445F2FB3-7B31-4005-88C2-4C38A9E5C142}](http://localhost:3001/wiki-media/1780044212038-685891645.png)
